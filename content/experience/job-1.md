## Lebanon Public Library

This was my first professional job after graduate school. It was an excellent learning opportunity that gave me real world experience in systems administration, networking, and customer service. I was responsible for maintaining the library's IT infrastructure, including servers, workstations, and network devices. I also provided technical support to library staff and patrons.

Lessons that I learned during this job are still useful today. Although I initially only had on prem systems, the networking, operating system and troubleshooting skills I developed have been invaluable in my career. I also learned the importance of documentation and communication, which are essential skills for any IT professional.
